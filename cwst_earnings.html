Casella continues to deliver double-digit top-line growth, with FY24 revenues reaching $1.55 billion (+23% YoY), driven heavily by strategic acquisitions and robust core pricing programs (>5%). Adjusted EBITDA margins remain healthy and expanding, reflecting operational discipline despite inflationary cost pressures.

The company has maintained strong pricing power in its core Solid Waste business, consistently achieving 5-6% price growth. While volumes have been slightly flattish due to purposeful shedding of lower-margin business, this strategy focuses on profitability and high-quality revenue streams.

‚öñÔ∏è Competitive Landscape

Casella (CWST) is a smaller, high-growth player compared to titans like Waste Management (WM) and Republic Services (RSG). While WM and RSG grow consistently in the mid-single digits (~5-8%), CWST is growing significantly faster (~15-20%) due to its aggressive acquisition strategy in the fragmented Northeast/Mid-Atlantic market. However, this growth comes at a premium valuation (>100x Forward P/E vs ~30x for peers), reflecting the market's expectation of continued rapid expansion and consolidation.

KPI Definitions

Adj EBITDA: Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization, adjusted for non-recurring items. Key metric for operational cash flow.
Core Price: Percentage change in revenue derived from price increases, excluding fuel surcharges and acquisitions.
Volume: Percentage change in revenue derived from changes in waste volume processed/collected.
Collection: Revenue from picking up waste from residential/commercial customers.
Disposal: Revenue from landfill and transfer station tipping fees.
